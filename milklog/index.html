MilkLog

Parenting

Track baby feeding on iPhone — formula, breast milk, and direct nursing — with quick entry, charts, home screen widgets, feeding reminders, optional iCloud sync, and Siri shortcuts.

Key Features

Quick logging

Log formula, pumped milk, or direct breastfeeding with amounts, duration, and sides — fast entry when you only have one hand free.

Charts & trends

See day, week, and month views to understand feeding patterns and daily totals at a glance.

Widgets & Siri

Glance at the last feed from the Home Screen and log with Siri Shortcuts using natural phrases.

Reminders & sync

Optional next-feed reminders, CSV export for your records, and optional iCloud sync across your iPhone (and data tied to your Apple ID).
