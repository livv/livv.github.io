Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

vPaste (referred to as "the App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Local Storage: Your clipboard history is stored locally on your device. We do not automatically upload this data to external servers.
  • Optional Sync: If you enable cloud sync, your clipboard data will be encrypted and stored securely. You can disable sync at any time.
  • Usage Analytics: With your consent, we may collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Crash Reports: If you opt-in, crash reports may be sent to help us diagnose and fix issues. These reports contain no personal information.

2. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's clipboard management functionality
  • To sync your clipboard history across your devices (if enabled)
  • To improve and optimize the App's performance
  • To diagnose and fix bugs and technical issues
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is our priority:

  • Local Data: By default, all clipboard data is stored locally on your Mac.
  • Encrypted Sync: If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted before transmission using industry-standard encryption.
  • Secure Practices: We follow industry best practices to protect any data that may be temporarily collected.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud sync infrastructure (all data is encrypted)
  • Anonymous analytics (non-identifying)
  • Crash reporting services

We do not sell or share your personal clipboard data with any third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can access all your clipboard history directly in the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual items or clear your entire history.
  • Export: You can export your saved snippets and folders.
  • Opt-Out: You can disable analytics, crash reporting, and cloud sync at any time.
  • Uninstall: Simply uninstall the App to remove all local data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
