Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

vClean (referred to as "the App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Local Data Only: The App processes files locally on your Mac. We do not upload, transmit, or store any of your files on external servers.
  • Usage Analytics: With your consent, we may collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Crash Reports: If you opt-in, crash reports may be sent to help us diagnose and fix issues. These reports contain no personal information.

2. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To improve and optimize the App's performance
  • To diagnose and fix bugs and technical issues
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is our priority:

  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any user data on cloud servers.
  • Local Processing: All file operations are performed locally on your device.
  • Secure Practices: We follow industry best practices to protect any data that may be temporarily collected.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with any third parties. The App does not include advertising, tracking, or analytics that would transmit data to external parties.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can access all features of the App directly on your device.
  • Deletion: Simply uninstall the App to remove all local data.
  • Opt-Out: You can disable analytics and crash reporting in the App's settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
